## Platform-specific tips

- **UrbanPro** — India-focused; fill every profile field (response rate + completeness drive
  ranking). Respond to enquiries fast — they expire.
- **Superprof** — strong for NRI/global searches; a profile photo + intro video noticeably
  raises bookings. First lesson free is expected here.
- **Preply** — global, commission-based; the intro video is basically required. Your hourly
  rate and fast reply time affect placement.
