# Lead-Generation Plan — Sri Telugu Classes

A concrete, do-it-this-month plan to turn strangers into booked free trials.
Pair with `outreach-kit.md` (the copy), `social-playbook.md` (where/when to post),
and `leads-tracker.csv` (where leads land).

> The numbers below are **starting targets** — adjust to your real teaching capacity
> (Jyothi + Vijaya). The structure matters more than the exact figures.

---

## 1. The goal & the funnel math

**Goal (Month 1):** ~**10 new enrolled students**.

Work backwards through realistic conversion rates for a warm, free-trial language class:

| Stage | Conversion | Needed / month | Needed / week |
|-------|-----------|----------------|---------------|
| Enrolled students | — | 10 | ~2–3 |
| Trial classes completed | ~50% trial→enroll | 20 | ~5 |
| Trials **booked** | ~80% show-up | 25 | ~6 |
| **Leads** (form/DM/comment) | ~50% lead→booked | **~50** | **~12** |

**So the whole machine runs on ≈ 50 leads/month ≈ 12/week ≈ 2 a day.** That's very
achievable across the channels below.

## 2. Where the 50 leads come from (channel mix)

| Channel | Target leads/mo | Cost | Effort |
|---------|----------------|------|--------|
| Facebook groups (organic posts + comments) | 15 | Free | High |
| Meta Ads (FB + Instagram) | 15 | $150–300/mo | Low once set |
| Instagram (Reels + DMs) | 8 | Free | Medium |
| Referrals from current students/parents | 7 | Free (1 free month) | Low |
| Marketplaces (UrbanPro/Superprof/Preply) | 5 | Free / per-lead | Low |
| **Total** | **~50** | | |

Don't spread thin — **Facebook + Meta Ads are the engine** (~60% of leads). The rest are
support. Double down on whatever actually converts after Week 2.

## 3. The 30-day launch calendar

### Week 0 — Setup (before posting anything)
- [ ] Confirm the trial form works end-to-end (email + Sheet) — test from phone on mobile data.
- [ ] Set up the lead tracker (import `leads-tracker.csv` to Google Sheets).
- [ ] Polish Instagram + Facebook pages: bio, free-trial link, 3–4 starter posts so the page
      doesn't look empty when people click through.
- [ ] Create a **Meta Business / Ads account** and install the Meta Pixel on the site (so ads
      can optimize for form submissions). *(Ask Claude to add the pixel snippet.)*
- [ ] Create profiles on UrbanPro, Superprof, Preply (copy in `marketplace-listings.md`).
- [ ] Record 3 short phone-video Reels (word of the day, a kid win, 60-sec mini-lesson).

### Week 1 — Go live, organic only
- [ ] Join the 7 NRI Telugu Facebook groups (`social-playbook.md`).
- [ ] Post **Value Post (A)** in 1 group/day, spaced out — never the same text twice same day.
- [ ] Publish 3 Instagram Reels (Mon/Wed/Fri) with the hashtag set.
- [ ] Reply to every comment/DM within a few hours; log each as a lead.
- [ ] Ask your 2–3 happiest current families for a testimonial + a referral.

### Week 2 — Add paid + marketplaces
- [ ] Launch **one Meta ad** ($5–10/day): audience = *parents · interest Telugu/Tollywood ·
      living in US/CA/UK/AU*; creative = your best Reel; CTA → trial form.
- [ ] Go live on the 3 marketplaces; respond to enquiries fast (they expire).
- [ ] Start showing up on **r/ABCDesis** — answer "how do I teach my kid Telugu" threads
      genuinely (no links yet, build karma).
- [ ] Rotate Facebook posts B and C across groups.

### Week 3 — Optimize
- [ ] Look at the tracker: **which Source converts best?** Pause losers, pour effort into winners.
- [ ] Scale the ad budget only on the winning ad/audience (e.g. $10 → $15/day).
- [ ] Launch the **referral offer** to every enrolled family ("refer a friend → both get a free month").
- [ ] Collect 3–5 reviews on your marketplace profiles + Google.

### Week 4 — Review & systematize
- [ ] Tally the month: leads, cost/lead, trials, enrollments, CAC (see KPIs below).
- [ ] Lock in the weekly routine that worked; cut what didn't.
- [ ] Plan next month's batch date + urgency ("Next batch starts ___").

## 4. The daily routine (~30–45 min)

1. **Check leads** (Gmail + Sheet) → reply to new ones first, book trials.
2. **Follow up** anyone due today (sort tracker by Follow-up Date).
3. **One organic post or 5 genuine comments** in a group/IG.
4. **Glance at the ad** — spend, cost per lead, pause if a lead costs > ~$20.

## 5. KPIs to watch (monthly)

| Metric | Target (Month 1) | Why |
|--------|------------------|-----|
| Leads | 50 | Top of funnel |
| Cost per lead (ads) | < $15 | Ad efficiency |
| Lead → trial booked | > 50% | Reply speed & offer quality |
| Trial show-up rate | > 80% | Reminders working |
| Trial → enrolled | > 40% | Teaching + fit |
| Enrollments | 10 | The goal |
| CAC (ad spend ÷ enrollments) | < $30 | Sustainable growth |

## 6. Owners

- **Jyothi & Vijaya** split lead replies + trials by timezone (India vs. US).
- One person owns the **tracker** so nothing falls through.
- Whoever posts also **replies to that thread's** comments same day.

## 7. The 3 rules that make or break it

1. **Speed wins.** Reply to a new lead within a few hours — conversion drops fast after a day.
2. **Value before ask.** In groups, help first; the soft CTA comes last. Spam gets you banned.
3. **Track the Source.** If you don't know which channel produced each lead, you can't double
   down. Fill the Source column every time.
