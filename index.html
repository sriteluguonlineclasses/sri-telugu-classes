FAQ

Questions parents ask us most

Everything you need to know before enrolling your child — or yourself.

We welcome learners of all ages — from children as young as 5 years old up to adults. Our Kids Special course is designed for ages 5–12 with stories, rhymes, and games. Teens and adults join our Telugu Basics, Reading & Writing, or Spoken Telugu tracks depending on their current level.

All live classes are conducted via Google Meet or Zoom — no extra software required. You only need a device with a camera and microphone. A stable internet connection is all it takes to join from anywhere in the world.

Life happens — we understand. Every live session is recorded and shared with enrolled students so your child never falls behind. You can also request a make-up slot with the teacher if you notify us at least 24 hours in advance.

We keep batches small on purpose — typically 6 to 10 students per class. Small groups mean your child gets personal attention, more speaking practice, and a genuine relationship with the teacher rather than getting lost in a crowd.

Most students notice a meaningful difference within 4–6 weeks of consistent attendance. Children typically start reading simple words and short sentences within the first month of Telugu Basics. For conversational fluency in Spoken Telugu, plan for 3–4 months of regular practice.

Yes — completely free, no credit card needed. During the 60-minute trial class, the teacher meets your child, assesses their current level, explains the course structure, and runs a mini interactive lesson. You'll know by the end whether it's the right fit.

Fees vary by course and are shared transparently after the free trial. There are no registration fees, no hidden charges, and no long-term contracts — you pay month to month. We also offer sibling discounts for families enrolling more than one child. Reach out via WhatsApp or email for the latest pricing.
